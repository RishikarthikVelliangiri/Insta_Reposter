Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Instagram Repost Tool ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following information:

  • Instagram account information (username, user ID) through OAuth authentication
  • Content you request to repost, including videos and captions
  • Technical information such as IP address and browser information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Enable you to repost content to your Instagram account
  • Improve and enhance our service
  • Communicate with you about service-related matters

Data Storage and Security

We store your Instagram authentication tokens securely. Content you repost is temporarily stored and then deleted after the reposting process is complete.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with Instagram and YouTube for content sharing purposes. Your use of these third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@instareposttool.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@instareposttool.com.
